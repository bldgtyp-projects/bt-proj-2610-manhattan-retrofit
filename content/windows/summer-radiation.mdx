- Thanks to the thick tree canopy in the rear yard, the south-facing glazing shows relatively low solar radiation. It is still 
    recommended to ensure that all south facing glazing be outfitted with robust user-operable solar shades.
- The highest summer radiation is apparent on the bulkhead level skylights. These skylights should be outfitted with solar shades to
    ensure against overheating risk in the upper levels.
- While the bulkhead's east-facing glazing receives modest solar radiation thanks to the shading from neighboring buildings, due to the low angle
    of the east sun it is recommended to include user-operable shades/blinds to ensure against discomfort glare, and to help with solar control.
