- Min. 3-1/2" EIFS Insulation ([HFO XPS](https://www.owenscorning.com/en-us/insulation/commercial/foamular-ngx), [Neopor Graphite EPS](https://neopor-insulation.com/) or sim.).
- Primary airtightness layer to be [Pro-Clima Intello Plus Membrane](https://475.supply/products/intello-plus) or sim. 
  'smart' vapor retarding airbarrier applied on the inside face of steel stud wall. Use 
  [Pro Clima Duplex](https://475.supply/products/duplex?_pos=2&_sid=5ccfa465a&_ss=r) double-sided tape for installing
  Intello over steel-stud framing.
- [3-1/2" mineral-fiber](https://www.owenscorning.com/en-us/insulation/commercial/thermafiber) batt insulation installed 
  to fill steel-stud cavities.
- 2x3 'service-cavity' wall insulated with formaldehyde-free mineral-wool 
  batt ([Thermafiber](https://www.owenscorning.com/en-us/insulation/commercial/formaldehyde-free) of sim.)
